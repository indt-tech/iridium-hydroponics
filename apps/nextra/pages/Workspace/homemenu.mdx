# Home menu
The workspace is the admin panel that allows you to customize your application and services. Here, you can create and manage your system entities, like gardens, controllers, etc.

On the left side you have a section preview with the managing content:

## 🪴​ My gardens
<div>
This section allows users to view and create their gardens with customizable names, enabling them to visualize and interact with the reported data.
</div>
<div>
![Protofy](http://localhost:8080/media/workspace/mygardens.jpg?raw=true)
</div>

## 🍓​ Garden types
<div>
Here, users can define different types of plantations and create new ones to set specific limits for nutrients, light, pH, etc., tailored to their plants' needs.
</div>
<div>
![Protofy](http://localhost:8080/media/workspace/gardentypes.jpg?raw=true)
</div>

## 📈​ Garden Events
<div>
This section logs all events that occur in the plantations, such as changes in pH, water level, and other relevant data.
</div>
<div>
![Protofy](http://localhost:8080/media/workspace/gardenevents.jpg?raw=true)
</div>

## ​🧠 My controllers
<div>
Users can manage their ESP32 devices here and add new ones as needed for monitoring and controlling their hydroponic systems.
</div>
<div>
![Protofy](http://localhost:8080/media/workspace/mycontrollers.jpg?raw=true)
</div>

## 💭 Controller setups
<div>
In this part, users can define their ESP32 devices along with the actuators and sensors they want to use. These configurations can then be applied in the "My controllers" section.
</div>
<div>
![Protofy](http://localhost:8080/media/workspace/controllersetups.jpg?raw=true)
</div>

## 🕹️​ Automations
<div>
Here, users can view a list of their automations related to nutrients, water, and other aspects. They can create or edit new automations according to their preferences and requirements.
</div>
<div>
![Protofy](http://localhost:8080/media/workspace/automations.jpg?raw=true)
</div>
